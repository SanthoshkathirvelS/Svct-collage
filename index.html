Academic Facilities

1

Internet

24/7 high-speed fiber optic lab for students, faculty & staff. Intranet for exam schedules, campus news, digital library access.

2

Canteen

Two canteens and a food court for hygienic meals. Subsidized mess for hostellers, separate facilities for males and females.

3

Laboratory

Well-equipped, spacious labs in all departments with latest equipment for practicals.

4

Transport

Comprehensive bus service for convenient and safe transportation, details on department website.

5

Hostels

Separate boys' & girls' hostels with computer access, gym, and 24/7 availability of deputy wardens & faculty.

CAD/CAM Centre

Training in AutoCAD, Pro-E, CATIA, ANSYS, etc., certification courses, machinery like STARTURN & STARKILL.
