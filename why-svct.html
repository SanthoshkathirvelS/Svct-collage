Placements

Our strong relationships with a diverse range of organizations, from global corporations to exciting startups, go beyond mere recruitment. We are committed to fostering mutual support and collaboration between students and companies in discovering their ideal matches and opportunities.

Career Development Cell

Training and upskilling students to meet up the skill demands of the future is our important objective. SVCT ensures that the training you receive during your college days not only helps you to land a job but also helps you to obtain long-term career growth. We cover a wide range of training modules that ensures that our students not only land their dream job but also transform into the most sought talented and skilled professionals in the industry.

Employability Boot camp

The Career Development Cell conducts one month of intensive and rigorous training for students of all years and in all departments, once a year to ensure that students are qualified to crack corporate interviews in the competitive market. During this boot camp, technical training on domains like Artificial Intelligence, Cyber security, and data Science is given to students with the right blend of interview skills, personality development, and aptitude training.

Eco Friendly Environment

A 22 Acre Green Campus spanning in the midst of Lush Green Environment surrounded by lakes and other natural environment providing calm and peaceful environment for good health, Eliminates Distractions and Apt for studying.

IT Infrastructure

IT Infrastructure A campus-wide wireless computing network is in place that allows faculty, staff, and students to log on to the Internet at any point in time. The Internet lab powered by dedicated leased lines (up to 50 Mbps) is open for students even after college hours to help them spend extra time making use of the abundant information available on the Internet
